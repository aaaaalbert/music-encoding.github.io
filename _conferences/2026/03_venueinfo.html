Venue Information

Welcome to the conference! We are delighted to host this year's event at the Morito Memorial Hall (森戸記念館) of the Tokyo University of Science.

Location & Map

The venue is located in the historic and charming Kagurazaka district.

Morito Memorial Hall, Tokyo University of Science
4-2-2 Kagurazaka, Shinjuku-ku, Tokyo

Access Information

Located centrally in Tokyo, the venue is easily accessible via a comprehensive network of public transportation.

From International Airports

  • From Narita Airport (NRT): Approximately 90 minutes by train.
  • From Haneda Airport (HND): Approximately 60 minutes by train.

Closest Stations

The closest stations to the venue are:

  • Toei Oedo Line: Ushigome-kagurazaka Station (A3 Exit) - 3 min walk
  • JR Line / Tokyo Metro: Iidabashi Station (JR West Exit / Metro B3 Exit) - 6 min walk
